## About this App:
This app aims to be able to summarize entire legal processes based on documents such as:
- Instrument Appeals
- Special Appeals
- Extraordinary Appeals
- Court Decisions
- Among others ..

### Features
- Summarization of legal processes using LLM and GenAI
- Backend orchestration using H2OGPTe
